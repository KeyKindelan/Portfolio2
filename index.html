Background

Before journalism, Frank spent 15 years researching land records throughout the Chicagoland region, managing a team of five and overseeing title insurance and escrow operations at O'Connor Title Guaranty. That foundation in document work and institutional paper trails directly shapes his approach to accountability reporting. He holds an MA from CUNY's Newmark J-School with a concentration in business and economics reporting, and a BS from Northwestern, Magna Cum Laude. He also plays drums.
